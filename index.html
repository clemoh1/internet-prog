The man who cracked the code and changed the world.

Alan Turing: Mathematician, Logician, and Codebreaker.

Timeline of a Genius:

  • 1912 - Born in London, England.
  • 1936 - Published "On Computable Numbers," inventing the "Turing Machine."
  • 1939 - Joined Bletchley Park to help crack the Enigma code during WWII.
  • 1950 - Proposed the "Turing Test" for Artificial Intelligence.
  • 1954 - Passed away, leaving a legacy that built the digital age.
